Rules of Color Revolution

1. The player will spin the wheel and receive tokens according to the color and number of the landing segment.


2. Sections of the wheel will have a color and number. The amount earned from the color and number will be multiplied together. For example, landing on Green 3 will give 2 * 3 or 6 tokens.


3. Ways to earn tokens:

- Red is worth 1 token

- Green is worth 2 tokens

- Blue is worth 3 tokens

- The number 3 is worth 1 token

- The number 2 is worth 2 token

- The number 1 is worth 3 token


4. It will cost 4.2 tokens to play once.

Theoretical Probability Distribution

1 Token (Red 3): 1 / 9 %


2 Tokens (Red 2 or Green 3): 2 / 9 %


3 Tokens (Red 1 or Blue 3): 2 / 9 %


4 Tokens (Green 2): 1 / 9 %


6 Tokens (Green 1 or Blue 2): 2 / 9 %


9 Tokens (Blue 1): 1 / 9 %
